Company Mission

Gulf Islands Consulting is a Victoria-based consulting firm composed of students in the UVic Engineering and Computer Science program that specializes in providing an easy link for companies of the Gulf Islands to the software industry of Victoria.

Our Client

Island Drywall & Finishing is a drywall company based out of Gabriola Island, BC. Island Drywall works on local construction projects on Gabriola and sometimes around the Nanaimo area. Project work often includes drywall, taping, insulation, vapour-barrier, and painting. With Island Drywall, customers can expect quality work done for a reasonable price, as is evident by past projects. Island Drywall & Finishing has been a reliable drywall service and has been trusted by locals since it was founded in 2009.

Our Client's Request

Island Drywall & Finishing has contacted us to improve the drywalling process starting with the estimation system. They are not satisfied with the time and resources required in the current process. They would like a faster and ideally remote system with the same accuracy to improve the estimation system.
